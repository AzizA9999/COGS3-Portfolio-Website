About Me

Hello, my name is Aziz Abdulahad and I'm studying Molecular & Cellular Biology at UCSD. Nice to meet you!

I enjoy reading philosphy and sci-fi novels, watching Anime, and going to the gym. I'm also a big foodie and try out every cuisine there is. I've recently gotten into Muay Thai and have also rekindled my long-rusty basketball skills from when I was young.

In terms of career goals, I want to pursue medicine and become a surgical oncologist to remove tumors from people. I have been surrounded by many people affected by cancer growing up and witnessed first-hand the suffering that comes with it. However, my desire to help people is not my only motivation. Before college, I actually had aspirations of becoming an enginner because I wanted to work with my hands. Researching the subject some more, I came to the conclusion that there was too much math and not enough free time. Therefore, I changed my focus to medicine because it still involved using my hands but came with the benefit of little to no calculations and loss of sanity.

To catapult my medical journey, I want to join a cancer reserach lab and study mechanisms underlying cancer metastasis and angiogenesis so that humanity can further conceptualize how cancer proliferates and thus develop new theraputics to fight back.
